Macaroni Cheese Recipe

Description

This is a recipe on how to make macaroni cheese.

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Butter
  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Melt butter
  3. Whisk in milk
  4. Stir in pasta
  5. Add seasoning
  6. Top with breadcrumbs
  7. Bake in oven
